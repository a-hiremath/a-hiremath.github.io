Balbir Singh Sodhi, a beloved Sikh father and Phoenix entrepreneur, was tragically killed on September 15, 2001, by a man who wrongly saw him as an enemy in the aftermath of 9/11. His death mourned deeply, reflects a heartbreaking tale of mistaken identity and loss.
